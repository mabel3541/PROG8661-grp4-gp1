Our Story

2020

The Beginning

TechSummit was founded with a vision to create the world's most impactful technology conference, bringing together the brightest minds in the industry.

2021

First Virtual Summit

Despite global challenges, we successfully hosted our first virtual summit, reaching over 10,000 participants worldwide and setting new standards for digital events.

2022

Hybrid Innovation

Pioneered the hybrid conference model, combining in-person networking with global digital participation, reaching 15,000 attendees across 50 countries.

Global Recognition

Recognized as "Conference of the Year" by Tech Industry Awards, featuring 200+ speakers and launching 50+ startup partnerships.

The Future Begins
